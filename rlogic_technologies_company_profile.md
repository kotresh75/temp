It is about the application of your knowledge/ideas that actually matters, and not just having ideas!

Here at Rlogic, we don’t just tell youwe demonstrate how things work. The videos are shot in real time while we build our applications. We bring you the latest and best in the industry so that you, as an individual, can perform better in the areas of application development.
